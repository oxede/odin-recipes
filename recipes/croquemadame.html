Croque Madame: French Elegance on Your Plate

Indulge in the luxurious flavors of a classic Croque Madame. This French bistro favorite features layers of creamy béchamel sauce, ham, and Gruyère cheese, all nestled between slices of toasted bread. The crowning glory is a perfectly fried egg on top, adding richness and flavor to every bite. It's a delightful dish that brings a touch of Parisian elegance to your breakfast or brunch table.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of white or sourdough bread
  • 4 slices of cooked ham
  • 1 cup Gruyère cheese, grated
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Start by making the béchamel sauce. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk constantly for about 2 minutes until it forms a smooth paste (a roux).
  2. Gradually add the milk while continuing to whisk. Cook the sauce, stirring, until it thickens and becomes smooth, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. If desired, season the béchamel sauce with nutmeg, salt, and pepper to taste. Remove it from the heat and set it aside.
  4. Preheat your oven's broiler.
  5. Toast the slices of bread on one side until lightly browned, then flip them over.
  6. Place a slice of ham on each slice of bread, followed by a generous amount of grated Gruyère cheese.
  7. Place the open-faced sandwiches under the broiler until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  8. While the sandwiches are broiling, in a separate pan, fry the eggs sunny-side up until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny.
  9. Once the sandwiches are done, remove them from the broiler and place a fried egg on top of each one.
  10. Serve your Croque Madame hot and enjoy!
