Chicken Pot Pie

Golden Comfort: Homemade Chicken Pot Pie Delight

This classic chicken pot pie is a comforting and hearty dish filled with tender chunks of chicken, mixed vegetables, and a creamy, savory sauce, all encased in a flaky golden crust. It's the perfect homemade comfort food for any occasion, and it's sure to satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, cubed
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 cup diced potatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 3/4 cups chicken broth
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 package refrigerated pie crusts (2 crusts)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add onions, potatoes, and carrots. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir in the flour, salt, pepper, and thyme until well combined.
  4. Gradually add the chicken broth and milk, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil.
  5. Add the cooked chicken and frozen mixed vegetables. Cook for a few more minutes until everything is heated through.
  6. Remove from heat.
  7. Line a pie dish with one of the pie crusts.
  8. Pour the chicken mixture into the pie crust.
  9. Place the second pie crust on top, and crimp the edges to seal. Cut a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  11. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
